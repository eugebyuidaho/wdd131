One of the most beautiful bookstores in the world

According to British newspaper The Guardian, El Ateneo Grand Splendid is the second best bookstore in the world. The Ateneo Grand Splendid opened in 1919, designed by architects Pero and Torres Armengol. Located in the Recoleta neighborhood, the Grand Splendid preserves the splendor and elegance of the former Grand Splendid theatre/cinema. This beautiful building was visited by famous artist such as Carlos Gardel, among others.

Why It Is Famous

This bookstore is internationally recognized because it transformed a historic theater into a literary and cultural space without losing its original beauty and character.

Architecture

Visitors can enjoy ornate balconies, decorative details, red stage curtains, and a remarkable painted ceiling that preserve the spirit of the original theater.

Visitor Experience

Guests can browse books, admire the interior, take photographs, and enjoy the café located in the former stage area.
